Call for Papers

We invite submissions of papers related to the AI applications/theories/datasets of cognitive and physical frailty, healthcare and aging, including but not limited to the following:

    Cognitive and Physical Frailty
  • Prevention
  • Detection
  • Primary Care
  • Invasive Procedures
  • Drug Treatments
  • Frailty Related Diseases
    • AI in Healthcare
  • Healthcare Delivery
  • Public Health
  • Social Well-being
  • Models of Care
  • Preventive Intervention
  • Rehabilitation
  • Dementia, 3H, Parkinson
    • AI in Aging
  • Ageless Aging
  • Virtual Companions
  • Unobtrusive Sensing
  • Persuasion Technology
  • Serious Games
  • Human-agent Interaction Design
  • Behavior Analysis and Modeling
  • Fall Detection and Prevention

Submissions should be be 2-4 pages (including references) in IJCAI 2021 format. The workshop committee will review the papers in a single-blind fashion. We encourage submissions of works that have been previously published or are to be presented in the main conference. Authors have the option to make their submissions non-archival, eliminating potential dual-submission conflicts with other venues.

All accepted papers will be included in a poster presentation session. We will select 2 submissions to each give an 25 minutes oral presentation. All accepted papers will be published in the proceedings.

Paper submission and review will be handled through CMT, at https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IJCAIAIF2021

Best Paper Prize

    To encourage participation, we set up the following awards:

  • Best Paper Award
  • Best Student Paper Award
  • Best Presentation Award

    • The award-winning authors or teams will be awarded certificates.
