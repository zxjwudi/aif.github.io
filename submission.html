    Submissions should be be 2-4 pages (including references) in IJCAI 2021 format. The workshop committee will review the papers in a single-blind fashion. We encourage submissions of works that have been previously published or are to be presented in the main conference. Authors have the option to make their submissions non-archival, eliminating potential dual-submission conflicts with other venues.

    All accepted papers will be included in a poster presentation session. We will select 2 submissions to each give a 25 minute oral presentation. All accepted papers will be published in our workshop proceedings.

    Paper submission and review will be handled through CMT, at https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IJCAIAIF2021

    Best Paper Prize

    To encourage participation, we set up the following awards:

    • Best Paper Award
    • Best Student Paper Award
    • Best Presentation Award

    The award-winning authors or teams will be awarded certificates.
